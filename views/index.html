How It Works

Expert Recommendations

Geojit Research uses technical analysis and quant models to generate stock ideas, recommending them to clients.

Convenient Notifications

Receive stock recommendations via WhatsApp until the maximum open recommendations for your wallet are reached.

Detailed Alerts

Each message includes stock name, wallet name, entry range, target range, stop loss, and expiry date.

Simple Approval

Approve orders directly through WhatsApp. Approved orders execute automatically when the specified ranges are met.

Automatic Square-off

Untriggered stocks auto square off at 3:10 PM on expiry day.

Optional Participation

Skip recommendations without approval, ensuring only the best opportunities reach you.
